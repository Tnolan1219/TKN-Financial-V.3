TRICARE Medical Plans

  • Prime: Managed care with Primary Care Manager (PCM); minimal out-of-pocket for active duty families.
  • Select: PPO-style flexibility with cost shares; available to most eligible beneficiaries.
  • Reserve Select / Retired Reserve: Premium-based options for Guard/Reserve.
  • Young Adult: For dependents up to age 26.
  • TRICARE For Life: Wraparound to Medicare for eligible retirees.

Dental & Vision

  • TRICARE Dental Program (TDP): For eligible family members; active duty dental is provided separately.
  • FEDVIP: Dental/vision available to retirees and eligible family members.
  • Vision coverage options vary by status and location.
  • Enrollment periods and eligibility rules apply‚Äîcheck annually.

SGLI / FSGLI / VGLI

  • SGLI: Up to $500,000 coverage for service members; low premiums; TSGLI rider included.
  • FSGLI: Covers spouses (premium paid by member) and dependent children at no cost.
  • TSGLI: One-time payments for qualifying traumatic injuries.
  • VGLI: Convert SGLI to renewable term coverage after leaving service; time limits apply for guaranteed acceptance.

Survivor Benefit Plan (SBP)

  • Lifetime monthly annuity to designated beneficiaries based on a percent of retired pay.
  • Premiums deducted from retired pay; election made at retirement.
  • Provides financial security for survivors after the member‚Äôs death.
  • Can be combined with VA Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC) in some cases.

Accessing Care & Claims

  • Update enrollment in milConnect after life events (PCS, marriage, birth).
  • Know referral rules (Prime) and network cost shares (Select).
  • Use TRICARE Online or call your regional contractor for appointments and claims.
  • Keep copies of orders, EOBs, and receipts for claims and appeals.
  • Contact your local medical group or base clinic for help with coverage or claims issues.

FAQ & Resources

  • How do I enroll or update TRICARE coverage? Use milConnect or contact your service branch.
  • What happens to SGLI after separation? You can convert to VGLI within time limits.
  • Can I have both SBP and VA DIC? Some survivors may be eligible for both; check current rules.
  • Where do I get help with claims? Contact your TRICARE contractor, base clinic, or local medical group.
  • Where can I find official info? See TRICARE, VA, DFAS, and your service branch websites.
