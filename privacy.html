Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Overview

Valentor Financial prioritizes privacy. This site is designed to keep your data local to your device when possible and minimize any collection of personal information.

What we store

  • Local-only data: calculator inputs, saved models, and profile preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage (on your device).
  • Optional exports: you may export JSON files containing your own data; those files are created locally and downloaded to your device.

What we don't do

  • We do not require account sign‑ups for core calculators.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data to third parties for marketing.

Third‑party services

Some pages may link to external sites or use third‑party resources (analytics, payment providers). Those external services have their own privacy policies; please review them before use.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or need data removed, contact: hello@milfi.app
